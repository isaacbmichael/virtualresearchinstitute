Online Mentorship

Research Pathways

Pathways into mentored research.

VRI offers two primary ways to participate. Circles provide a broader public entry point, while Groups offer a smaller and more intensive cohort experience.

Tier I

Circles

Structured cohort-based experiences that introduce students to serious mentor-guided research in an accessible but rigorous format.

Tier II

Groups

Smaller collaborative cohorts with closer mentor interaction, deeper sustained work, and a more intensive research experience.

Limited Private Mentorship may be available in select cases as an advanced continuation pathway when prior performance, mentor recommendation, project suitability, and capacity align.
