Mentored Research in Mathematics and Computational Sciences

Mentored research for ambitious young scholars.

The Virtual Research Institute provides a structured and intellectually ambitious home for high-school students pursuing research in mathematics, statistics, data science, and computational fields. Built around close mentorship and clear academic standards, VRI is designed to feel less like a course and more like an institute.

About

An online home for thoughtful student research.

VRI brings together close mentorship, high academic standards, and a thoughtful scholarly environment for students in mathematics and the computational sciences.

What makes VRI distinctive

VRI is designed for students who are ready for more than enrichment: close mentorship, sustained intellectual work, and a clear pathway into authentic research culture.

About VRI

Research Pathways

Two public pathways, plus limited private mentorship.

Circles and Groups provide different levels of scale and mentor access, with limited Private Mentorship available by inquiry for select matches and longer-term work.

Tier I

Circles

Structured and scalable entry-tier experiences that introduce students to research habits in an accessible but still rigorous environment.

Tier II

Groups

Smaller collaborative cohorts with more intensive mentor interaction, stronger ownership, and a more boutique research experience.

By Inquiry

Private Mentorship

Limited individualized placements for select matches, specialized topics, and longer-term mentor-guided work.
