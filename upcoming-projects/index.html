Overview

A simpler way to review current offerings.

Start with the pathway, not the full proposal list. Circles are the broader public entry point. Groups are smaller and more intensive. Private Mentorship is limited, individualized, and arranged by inquiry.

Tier I

Circles

Structured and scalable mentored research experiences designed to introduce students to serious research habits in an accessible but still rigorous environment.

10–12 students 6 weeks $1,395
  • Broader public entry point
  • Two public sessions
  • Strong option for students building research readiness
Circles include two public entry points so families can choose the timing that best fits the student.
View Circles
Tier II

Groups

Smaller collaborative cohorts with more intensive mentor interaction, greater ownership, and a closer resemblance to a premium boutique research experience.

5–6 students 10 weeks $3,495
  • Smaller and more intensive than Circles
  • One public cohort
  • Better suited to students ready for deeper sustained work
Group placement may involve additional readiness and fit review for the pathway and topic.
View Groups

Private Mentorship

Need a more individualized research match?

For students seeking more individualized or longer-term work, VRI may occasionally consider limited Private Mentorship placements by inquiry.

By inquiry only

Starting tuition: $16,000. Availability depends on subject area, mentor fit, student readiness, and researcher availability. Scheduling, pacing, and meeting schedules may differ by mentor and project.
