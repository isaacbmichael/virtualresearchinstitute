# Mentor Onboarding Checklist

**Document status:** Internal draft for operations and onboarding  
**Prepared for:** Virtual Research Institute LLC  
**Intended use:** Working checklist for mentor approval, onboarding, and activation

---

## 1. Purpose

This checklist is intended to help VRI confirm that a mentor has completed the key approval, documentation, payment, compliance, and readiness steps required before beginning work under VRI.

## 2. Status of Document

This draft is a working internal operational document and may be revised as VRI’s onboarding process evolves.

## 3. Core Onboarding Items

The following items should generally be completed before a mentor is activated for VRI work:

- signed Mentor Independent Contractor Agreement;
- signed confidentiality / non-circumvention terms or equivalent provisions;
- completed tax documentation (such as Form W-9, if applicable);
- approved payment setup for direct deposit;
- verified legal name and contact information;
- verified location and time zone;
- reviewed VRI communication, professionalism, and safeguarding expectations;
- reviewed documentation and recordkeeping expectations.
