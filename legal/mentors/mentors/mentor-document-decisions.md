# Mentor Document Decisions

**Document status:** Internal working notes  
**Purpose:** Central source for business decisions that should stay aligned across mentor-facing legal and onboarding drafts

---

## Current Working Decisions

### 1. Engagement model
- Mentors are intended to serve as **independent contractors (1099 model)** unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.

### 2. Agreement term
- Current preferred term: **1 year**
- Renewal: **discretionary**, based on program need, performance, compliance, and mutual fit

### 3. Payment model
- Payment method: **direct deposit**
- Compensation structure:
  - **Circles:** $5,000 per cohort
  - **Groups:** $6,500 per cohort
  - **Private Mentorship:** case-based
    - $3,000 for one student
    - $5,500-$6,000 total for two students

### 4. Private Mentorship structure
- Private Mentorship is **not guaranteed**
- It is generally intended only for **select continuation cases**
- Mentor must not assume Private Mentorship is available every term

### 5. Screening and activation
- Mentor approval is **discretionary**
- VRI may require identity verification, reference review, publication/credential review, and background checks where appropriate
- Final activation should occur only after required documentation and review are complete

### 6. Protection priorities
- Confidentiality / NDA protection
- Non-circumvention / no private side deals
- Non-solicitation / no-poaching
- Brand / template / workflow protection
- No unauthorized statements on behalf of VRI

### 7. Public vs. private
- Public For Mentors page should summarize the framework at a high level
- Signed agreements and onboarding documents should contain the stronger enforceable details
