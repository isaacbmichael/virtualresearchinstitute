Overview

A curated archive of completed student research.

The archive helps families, students, mentors, and institutional partners see the range and quality of research work produced through VRI.

Projects

Titles and abstracts

Entries may include project titles, abstracts, topic areas, terms, mentors, and student display names when approved for public release.

Materials

Presentations and artifacts

Selected entries may include slides, posters, recordings, papers, code repositories, or other approved project materials.

Search

Find projects by topic

Visitors will be able to search by year, mentor, student display name, project title, abstract, topic area, or keyword.

Archive Standards

Student work is published with care.

Research materials may include student names, mentor names, recordings, slides, project drafts, or other materials requiring review before public release. VRI treats the archive as a curated academic record.

Archive principle

Public entries should be accurate, permissioned, and professionally presented. Projects may be omitted, anonymized, summarized, or held for later review when public release is not appropriate.
